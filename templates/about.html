Accessible Game

You can control the game with the keyboard or voice control / handtracking.

Keyboard Control:
  • Start: Space or Eneter
  • Quit: Esc
  • Level Up/Down: Up/Down Arrow
  • Move Paddle: Left/Right Arrow
  • Sound: Click Sound checkbox
  • Accessibility: Click Accessibility checkbox
    • Voice/Accessible Control:

    Voice control for basic menu functionality is always available. To activate accessibility mode, say "accessibility"

    Accessibility mode will start a webcam hand tracking device. This is aided by a visualization.

    Move your hand to the left to move the paddle left, and to the right to move it right.

    Moving your hand to the middle of the screen or from the field of view will stop the paddle

  • Start: Say, "launch" or "start"
  • Quit: Say, "stop" or "quit"
  • Level Up/Down: Say, "level up" or "level down"
  • Sound: Say, "sound"
  • Accessibility: Say, "accessibility"
